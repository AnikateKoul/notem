Notem
  • Welcome to Notem!
  • Notem is a website you can use to create and save elegant notes
  • Drag over the canvas to create a note
  • Hold and move the bar on the top of your note to move it over the canvas
  • Click on the 'x' button on the top-left of the note to delete it
  • Use the four buttons on the navbar to customize the colour of the notes
  • Enjoy!
